About

Tom Cronin is a meditation teacher, author, and founder of The Stillness Project. After 26 years in finance as one of Sydney's leading bond and swap brokers, Tom experienced a personal transformation through meditation that led him to leave the corporate world and dedicate his life to sharing stillness with others.

Tom has facilitated over 180 retreats across Australia, Bali, Thailand, and Europe. His corporate wellness programs have been delivered to organizations including Google, Westpac, and Qantas. He is the producer of the documentary "The Portal" and author of "The Portal: How Meditation Can Save the World."
